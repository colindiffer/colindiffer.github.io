Account Deletion — Yonder Podcast

App: Yonder Podcast  |  Developer: Colin Differ

You can delete your Yonder Podcast account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the app. No email or external request is needed.

How to delete your account

1
Open Yonder Podcast on your device.
2
Tap the Account tab.
3
Scroll down and tap Delete account.
4
Confirm the deletion when prompted. Your account and all cloud data will be permanently removed.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently deleted immediately:

  • Your account credentials (Firebase Authentication record)
  • Your cloud-synced podcast subscriptions and library
  • Your cloud-synced app settings and per-podcast settings
  • Your cloud-synced played history and listening state

What data is kept

The following data stays on your device after account deletion and is not affected:

  • Downloaded episode files stored locally on your device
  • Local app data (AsyncStorage) such as local settings and playback position

This local data can be removed by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app from your device.

No account? No data stored

If you use Yonder Podcast without creating an account, no personal data is stored on our servers. All data is local to your device.
